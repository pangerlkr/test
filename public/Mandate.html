Functions of the Commission

NL-NSCW Act, 2006 — Section 5

01

Codify Customary Laws

Study, research and codify customary laws relating to Naga women, family institutions, and social security across tribes and districts.

02

Legislative Reorientation

Study and report on the advisability of reorienting customary laws and practices into legislative enactments.

03

Parliamentary Acts & Article 371-A

Research Acts of Parliament (CPC, CrPC, IPC) regarding their applicability to Naga women under Article 371-A.

04

State Legislative Analysis

Analyze all Acts of the Nagaland Legislative Assembly affecting women to plug loopholes and improve effectiveness.

05

Constitutional Safeguards

Advise on specific provisions in the Constitution of India for protecting the interests of women in Nagaland.

06

Policy Formulation

Formulate alternative policies for the socio-economic and educational advancement of women in Nagaland.

07

Monitoring Conditions

Examine and monitor the general conditions of women across the state.

08

Workplace Evaluation

Evaluate conditions for women in both private and public workplaces.

09

Custodial Inspections

Inspect Jails, Remand Homes, and custody centers to ameliorate conditions against human rights violations.

10

Grievance Redressal

Receive, investigate and report on complaints relating to the status and affairs of women.

11

General Empowerment

Perform all other functions incidental to the empowerment and welfare of women.

Section 5(2): It shall also be the duty of the Commission to conduct special inquiries into matters affecting welfare and empowerment as referred by the State Government.

Powers of the Commission

NL-NSCW Act, 2006 — Section 10

While performing investigative and inquiry functions under Section 5, the Commission shall have the powers of a civil court trying a suit, and in particular:

record_voice_over

Summoning and enforcing attendance of any person from any part of India and examining them on oath.

text_snippet

Requiring the discovery and production of any documents.

verified

Receiving evidence on affidavits.

folder_shared

Requisitioning any public record or copy thereof from any court or office.

history_edu

Issuing Commissions for the examination of witnesses and documents.

more_horiz

Any other matter which may be prescribed by the State Government.

Section 10(2): The Commission may, for the purpose of conducting investigations and inquiry under this Act, utilize the services of any officer of the State Government with its prior approval.

Section 10(3): The other procedural details in regard to conduct of investigations and inquiries shall, as far as possible, be the same as the procedure followed by a Civil Court.

Need to file a grievance?

The Commission accepts complaints from individuals relating to violations of women's rights.
