Data Privacy

We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. Our digital systems are architected to prioritize anonymity where possible and absolute security where data collection is necessary for advocacy and support.

All personal identifiers are encrypted using industry-standard protocols before being stored within our secure government-monitored infrastructure.
