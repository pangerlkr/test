Narrative of Change

The Nagaland State Commission for Women stands as a resolute sentinel for the rights and dignity of women across our hills. We don't just advocate; we curate a future of equity.

Our mission is to investigate and monitor all matters relating to the safeguards provided for women under the Constitution and other laws, ensuring justice is not just a promise, but a lived reality.
